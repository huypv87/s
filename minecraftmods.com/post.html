Road Mod

Minecraft 1.8 papertazer Requirements: Forge

The Road Mod is a great way to make proper roads and sidewalks in Minecraft while also adding signs, barriers and traffic lights!

Asphalt blocks are slightly shorter than the standard block height, which allows you to create curbs and sidewalks for a more realistic look. Sidewalks are similar to stone blocks but not quite the same. They are also standard height blocks. And curbs are simply attached to asphalt but are their own blocks.

You can even make the proper road markings for added effect. And while you’re add it, add those stop signs, traffic lights, and construction barriers too!

This mod goes great with any sort of vehicle mod that adds cars. Now you can properly drive through your cities in a more realistic fashion. Become your own Department of Transportation!
